About Bishop.

I'm Bishop Odedeyi, a self-taught developer and creative technologist driven by curiosity and innovation. My journey in tech has been fueled by world-class open learning resources like Harvard’s CS50 and MIT OpenCourseWare, where I built a strong foundation in computer science, AI, and cybersecurity.

My achievements include completing several hands-on CS50 problem sets, developing full-stack applications, contributing to open-source projects, and continuously expanding my skill set through real-world experimentation. I believe in lifelong learning, building solutions that matter, and leaving every project better than I found it.
